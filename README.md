## How to Play

1. **Enter VR** - Click the VR button or put on your headset
2. **Look at asteroids** - Target them with your gaze (green crosshair)
3. **Hold gaze for 0.5s** - Asteroid explodes!
4. **Score points** - Smaller asteroids = more points
5. **Level up** - Destroy asteroids to advance levels

## Controls

- **VR Mode**: Look around with your head, gaze at targets
- **Desktop**: Mouse to look, click on asteroids
- **Quest**: Works with or without controllers!
